Built for story development

Features that move ideas toward a screenplay.

MyConcept.Cloud gives screenwriters a focused place to capture ideas quickly, organize story pieces, and shape concepts into plots, scenes, and outlines.

Core features

Everything is centered around one goal: helping writers turn loose ideas into structured stories.

Quick Idea Capture

Capture story sparks the moment they happen — concepts, dialogue, character ideas, twists, scenes, endings, and anything else worth saving.

Concept Development

Expand a rough thought into a stronger story concept by organizing the core premise, conflict, tone, world, and direction.

Plot Building

Shape major beats, turning points, conflicts, reveals, and story progression so the idea starts becoming a real narrative.

Scene Organization

Break the story into scenes and moments that can be rearranged, refined, and connected into the larger plot.

Outline Creation

Bring your concepts, plot points, and scenes together into a clear outline that can guide the screenplay writing process.

Focused Writing Workspace

Keep the app centered on story structure instead of generic notes, random folders, or scattered documents.

Designed around the writing flow

Start messy. Finish structured.

Ideas rarely arrive fully formed. MyConcept.Cloud is designed to catch the rough version first, then help you shape it into something organized enough to write from.
