Features Built for Writers Who Actually Write

MyConcept.Cloud is your idea vault + story builder: capture concepts fast, expand them into scenes, organize characters, and search your entire creative brain in seconds.

Core Tools

Everything here is designed to keep your ideas from dying in your Notes app.

Concept Inbox

Capture ideas instantly—title, a quick blurb, and optional tags. Organize now or later. Nothing gets lost.

Scene Builder

Turn “what if…” into an actual scene: genre, setting, shot notes, characters, and a scene goal.

Character Profiles

Track who your characters are, what they want, relationships, and evolution—without a 40-tab spreadsheet.

Story Structure

Expand scenes into a full outline: 3-act structure, beats, arc notes, and story goals you can actually follow.

Tags & Filtering

Tag by genre, tone, location, theme, or project. Build a library you can search like Google.

Fast Search

Find “that one idea about the diner in the rain” instantly. Search across concepts, scenes, and characters.

What You Can Do, In Plain English

Less “feature list,” more “how it helps you finish your script.”

Capture ideas in 10 seconds

Log your concept before traffic, emails, or life steals it. Organize when you’re ready.

Grow a concept into scenes

Add setting, shot notes, tone, and character involvement so your idea becomes usable on writing day.

Turn scenes into an outline

Connect scenes into beats and a structure that feels like a real project—because it is.

Keep your ideas safe

You own your content. You can export it. Your work isn’t “training data” for someone else’s product.
