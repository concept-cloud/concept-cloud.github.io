Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 24, 2026

Plain-English Summary: You own your ideas. We don’t sell your data. We collect only what we need to run the service.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how MyConcept.Cloud (“MyConcept,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in three main ways:

  • Information you provide: name, email address, waitlist details, and any notes you submit.
  • Account data (if/when accounts exist): username, profile settings, and writing projects you create.
  • Usage data: basic analytics like pages visited, device/browser type, and approximate location (city/region).

3. Your Creative Content

If you store creative content (concepts, scenes, characters, outlines), you retain ownership of that content. We do not claim ownership over your work.

We use your content only to provide the service (saving, organizing, searching, and exporting), and to troubleshoot issues if you request support.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Communicate with you (beta access, updates, support replies)
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not claim ownership of your concepts or writing.
  • We do not publish your content publicly unless you explicitly choose to share it.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share information only in limited cases:

  • Service providers: hosting, databases, email delivery, analytics (only what’s necessary).
  • Legal obligations: if required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights/safety.
  • Business changes: merger/acquisition (you will be notified if this happens).

7. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your data.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we design with security in mind.

10. Your Choices

  • Opt out of marketing emails (unsubscribe link)
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information
  • Export your content (when supported)

11. Children’s Privacy

MyConcept.Cloud is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and may provide additional notice.
