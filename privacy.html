Privacy & Ownership

Your ideas belong to you.

Privacy Policy

MyConceptCloud

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Overview

MyConceptCloud is a native creative platform designed for screenwriters to capture ideas, build plots, organize scenes, and structure stories.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected while using the website, waitlist, and future applications.

2. Information We Collect

If you join the waitlist, we may collect your name, email address, writing type, experience level, and optional messages you provide.

Future versions of the platform may collect account information and creative content necessary to provide application features.

3. Creative Ownership

Your ideas, concepts, scenes, outlines, dialogue, characters, and creative works remain yours.

MyConceptCloud does not claim ownership over user-created story material.

4. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to: provide updates, manage early access, improve platform features, communicate with users, and operate MyConceptCloud.

5. Waitlist & Email Updates

Users who join the waitlist may receive emails related to: product updates, beta access, launch announcements, and development news.

6. Data Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect information, though no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

Trusted third-party services may be used for hosting, analytics, waitlist management, communications, and application infrastructure.

8. Access & Deletion Requests

Users may request access to or deletion of applicable personal information associated with their account or waitlist submission.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may evolve as MyConceptCloud develops. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

For privacy-related questions, contact:
