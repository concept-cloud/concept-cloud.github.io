Privacy

Your ideas belong to you.

MyConcept.Cloud is designed around trust. Your concepts, notes, plots, scenes, and outlines should stay private and under your control.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

1. Overview

MyConcept.Cloud is a native app for screenwriters to capture ideas and develop them into plots, scenes, and outlines. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected.

2. Information We Collect

If you join the waitlist, we may collect your name, email address, writing type, experience level, and any optional message you choose to provide.

When the app becomes available, we may collect account information and app content you create, such as concepts, notes, scenes, plots, and outlines, only as needed to provide the app’s features.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide updates, manage early access, improve the app, support users, and operate MyConcept.Cloud.

4. Your Creative Work

Your ideas, concepts, notes, plots, scenes, outlines, and other creative material remain yours. MyConcept.Cloud does not claim ownership of your creative work.

5. Email and Waitlist Updates

If you join the waitlist, we may email you about product updates, beta access, launch information, and related MyConcept.Cloud news. You may unsubscribe from these emails when that option is available.

6. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted third-party services to operate the website, manage waitlist submissions, send emails, host data, or provide app functionality.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no online service, website, or app can guarantee complete security.

8. Data Access and Deletion

You may request access to or deletion of personal information associated with your waitlist submission or account when applicable.

9. Children’s Privacy

MyConcept.Cloud is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as MyConcept.Cloud develops. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@myconcept.cloud
